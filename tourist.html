Tourist Attraction

Nagaon is a historical of Assam. the top atraction to visit in Nagaon district are: Kaziranga national Park, Bordowa Satra, Laokhowa Wildlife Sanctuary. Below i have mentioned some of them

  • Bordowa Satra

    Bordowa Satra is a popular centre of culture and art located in Nagaon.It is the birth place of a great literature, artist, dramatist and founder of Vaishnav Dharma in Assam.The Bordowa Satra is founded by great saint Shankardeva after his return from his first pilgrimage in about 1494 A.D. He found the first Namghar or Kirtanghar at Bordowa and used that place to practice and peace the newly found faith in Puran and Bhagawat.

  • Kaziranga National Park

    Kaziranga Nationa park is a national park in the Nagaon,Karbi anglong and Golaghat districts of assam. Kazironga National Park is the name to exemplify the most popular conservation efforts to save the endangered species like one-horned rhinoceros in India.

    Located just 25km from Nagaon town of assam the Laokhowa Wildlife Sanctuary is a home to innumerable species of birds,animals and plants. its main atraction is the Great Indian One Horned Rhinocerous
